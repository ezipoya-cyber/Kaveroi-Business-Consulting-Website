About Us

Kaveroi Business Consulting (KBC) was established with a simple but powerful vision: to make business support services more accessible and practical for everyday Papua New Guineans.

The journey of KBC began as a passion project, with the Managing Director providing consulting and business support services as a side venture for two years. Through this experience, it became clear that there was a significant gap between government regulatory agencies and the people they serve. Many individuals, small businesses, and entrepreneurs faced challenges in understanding and navigating business requirements and compliance processes.

Recognising this need, Kaveroi Business Consulting officially established its office in February 2026 and is now fully operational, providing professional consulting services designed to bridge this gap.

At KBC, we are committed to helping businesses, organisations, and individuals achieve sustainable growth through practical solutions, strategic advice, and professional support. We work to simplify processes and provide guidance that enables our clients to confidently engage with key government institutions, including the Investment Promotion Authority (IPA) and the Internal Revenue Commission (IRC), while also supporting broader business development needs.

Our expertise spans business consulting, business registration and compliance support, project management, training and capacity building, documentation and report preparation, taxation and regulatory guidance, administrative support services, and operational improvement. Whether you are starting a new business, expanding an existing enterprise, or seeking specialised professional support, KBC is dedicated to helping you succeed.

At the heart of our business is a commitment to building lasting relationships based on trust, transparency, and accountability. We believe that our clients' success is our success, and we strive to provide solutions that create long-term value and positive impact for businesses and communities across Papua New Guinea.

Our Mission

To empower businesses and communities by delivering innovative, reliable, and practical consulting services that drive growth, efficiency, and sustainable development while making business services accessible to all Papua New Guineans.

Our Vision

To become Papua New Guinea's trusted consulting partner, recognised for bridging the gap between government institutions and the people, fostering entrepreneurship, and creating opportunities for businesses to thrive.
